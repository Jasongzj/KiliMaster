Experience the cultural insight with us, to take you to a journey among the Maasai and the Hadzabe whose presence here makes your journey a stirring and soulful destination. KiliMaster would carefully select the cultural tourism destinations, which bring benefits to the village and ensure that will not give any negative influences on these cultures.

Lake Eyasi:
the Hadzabe tribe visit

Lake Eyasi is the entrance to Ghorofani, in the area of Lake Eyasi, it lives the Hadzabe tribe. You can join the Hadzabe tribe or the Datoga communities on a hunting trip with traditional weapons, bow and arrows. Visiting these tribe can let you to see people who lives in a very traditional way which is far away from the modern life.

The Coffee Tour

The coffee tour is very popular in town. Through the coffee tour, it can not only shows you how beans are grown, picked and roasted. But offers insights into the lives of the Chagga coffee farmers who live on Kilimanjaro’s lower slopes. You can also have meals with local families and waterfall walks

Maasai village

The Maasai are most well known African tribes and can easily recognize them from their red Shuka. There are many Maasai villages, KiliMaster can give you a unique experiences on introducing to the Maasai life including a visit to some bomas and the Wednesday cattle market. The visit of these cultural tours can help to support the community and provide income to local families. And it is also a good way to get in touch with local cultures and traditions.

Safaris

Over the past two years a distinctive SafariBookings taskforce searched for those who have been on safari and invited them to write a review. For the result, over 1,000 people from 53 nationalities wrote more than 2,300 user reviews about safari. Reputable guidebook authors - working for Lonely Planet, Rough Guides, Frommer’s, Bradt and Footprint - teamed up in the SafariBookings Expert Panel and wrote 756 expert reviews. The overall average rating of Tanzania in both the user and expert reviews is 4.8 out of 5 stars: the highest score of all 8 major safari countries. Tanzania is one of the best safari destinations for hunting and photography.

Tanzania is the best country in Africa for buffalo, lion and leopard to live. The plains also are heaven for other animals such as gerenuk, lesser kudu, sable, roan and sitatunga.

With its abundant wildlife, excellent species diversity and evocative acacia and baobab-studded landscapes, you can be ensured an amazing holiday that you will remember for your entire life.
